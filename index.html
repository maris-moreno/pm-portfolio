Hi, I'm Maris!

A people-first project manager who builds calm, clear systems — with heart.

Growing up, I was always encouraged to think about efficiency — how to make the most of our time, how to solve a problem without making it bigger. Over time, that mindset stuck.

But I've also learned that behind every project is a person — or often, many. And the best systems don't just get things done — they support, empower, and make things feel a little lighter.

Whether I'm coordinating housing move-outs or managing timelines across creative projects, I'm always asking: What would make this easier? Clearer? Kinder?

That's what I bring to every project — thoughtful structure, clear communication, and a lot of heart.

I'm also a copywriter who loves trying new crafts, obsessing over puzzles, tending to my garden, traveling to new places, and mentoring kids in my community.
