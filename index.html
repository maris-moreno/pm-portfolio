Hi, I'm Maris!

A copywriter turned project manager who genuinely loves solving problems with organized, creative systems.

I grew up in a household where efficiency was a top priority. At the grocery store, at home, at school, I was always encouraged to think about how to do things as quickly and as easily as possible. Over time, that mindset stuck.

But as I've worked on more real-world projects, I've learned that the best systems aren't just built for getting things done, they're built for people too.

Whether I'm coordinating housing move-outs or juggling multiple creative timelines, I want to make sure everyone's on the same page, communication is flowing, and the systems in place are designed with people in mind.

That's what I bring to every project: clarity, communication, and kindness — so everyone has the tools they need to do their best work.

Some other things I love: trying new crafts, obsessing over puzzles, tending to my garden, traveling to new places, and mentoring kids in my community.
